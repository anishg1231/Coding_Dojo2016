About Me

Hello! My name is Anish Gheewala. I was first exposed to programming in high school where I took a basic C++ course. To my surprise, I was able to make something from nothing within a matter of hours. It was truly rewarding and eye opening for a young man. Sadly, during college I did not take the path of become a programmer, but received a degree in business management. In addition, I was worked at Walgreens, but after several years it dawned on me that I could not work retail any more. I needed to do something.

The saying goes that nothing worth having is easy and it isn’t. Over a year ago, I decided to teach myself basic HTML/CSS, while working as a full time store manger. Trust me when I say that some nights I just wanted to give up or say “there is always tomorrow.” However, the will I had to become a programmer was stronger then the desire to sleep sometimes. Every night, even if it was just a few hours, I would come home and build something or even learn something new. After accomplishing 100 plus hours learning HTML/CSS, I wanted to learn more! I tried teaching myself Ruby to see if I could learn more difficult languages, and I was able to learn the basics. At that point I knew programming was something I wanted to do and to make it my career.

I started to explore different options that’ll help me transition into a Software Developer and took my time to research different boot camps that was the best fit for me. I choose Coding Dojo, where the program allowed me to learn three Full Stack in fourteen week. I’ve learned Python with Django, MEAN (which included MongoDB, Express, AngularJS, and Node), and lastly Ruby/Ruby on Rails. Also, the daily algorithm practice on whiteboards at the Dojo made me a better problem-solver and thinker.
